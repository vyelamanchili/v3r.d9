Software Engineers' Association, SEA, is the Association of the Computer Science (CSE & CSIT) Department of
BHARAT INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY, Ibrahimpatnam.

With Mr.Rajesh Kulkarni as the Head of Department we formed an Association called Software Engineers' Association (SEA) started on 4th of March 2005 with chief guest as Mr.JanikiRam, Academic Developer and Evangelist from Microsoft.

All the CSE & CSIT of the college are the members of SEA.

All the events conducted by SEA are under the supervision of
Mr.Rajesh Kulkarni (H.O.D. of Computer Science Department), and Professor Rajesh Kapur and the Office Bearers of the Association.
