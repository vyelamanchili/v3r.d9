Software Engineers Association, SEA, is the Association of the Computer Science (CSE & CSIT) Department of BHARAT INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY, Ibrahimpatnam.


It was formed to organize technical and cultural events in and out of the college. SEA conducts competitions, useful Seminars and Events for all the students of the college.
