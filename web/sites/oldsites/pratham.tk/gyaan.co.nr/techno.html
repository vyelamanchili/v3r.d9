We take great pride in introducing ourselves, the students of BHARAT INSTITUTE OF ENGINERING & TECHNOLOGY (BIET) are conducting a TECHNICAL SYMPOSIUM on the 4th and 5th of March 2005 called the GYAAN 2005.With Prof. E.Ravindhran Ethiraj as the head of department we have formed an Association called Association of Communication Engineers (ACE) started on 20th of October 2004 With chief guest as R.Narsimha Reddy, the G.M of Bharat Dynamics Limited and guest of honor as the H.O.D of ECE department Osmania University.

ACE is a society formed for developing the individual talents in aspects regarding education and extracurricular activities that gives students a scope to apply their knowledge and skills and helps them to involve as a part of team that works dynamically.

Activities carried out by ACE: -

  • Field trips
  • Paper Presentations
  • Mini projects
  • Debates
  • Seminars
  • Social service
  • Technical quizzes

This Site contains all the information required to participate in the Event.
